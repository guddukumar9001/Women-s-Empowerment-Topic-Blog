Women's Empowerment Topic Blog

Women's Empowerment

Women's empowerment means providing women with the rights and opportunities to make decisions and shape their lives.

Freedom of expression

Women's empowerment includes the ability to express themselves freely without fear of discrimination.

Decision-making

Women's empowerment includes the ability to make decisions about their own lives, including their health, education, and career.

Economic participation

Women's empowerment includes equal pay and recognition of women's unpaid care work.

Child marriage

Women's empowerment includes addressing child marriage, which is a barrier to women's education.
